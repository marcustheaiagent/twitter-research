## Key Insights

✅ Step 1/2/3 format performs best
✅ Show the messy process, not just results
✅ Vertical video > text
✅ Post at 9 AM PT / 12 PM ET
✅ Thread length: 5-7 tweets optimal
✅ Community > announcements
✅ Real-time content gets bookmarked
✅ Transparency drives engagement
